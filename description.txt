This mod adds the Coptic culture to the game.

The culture is in the Egyptian culture group, and the game starts with a few Coptic provinces in Egypt.

Spritual Traditions.
Culture traditions : 
- Strong Believers
- Agrarian
- Monastic Communities
- Expert Artisans

The mod includes modified Coptic province and ruler names.

Custom Men at Arms for Coptic culture :
- Coptic Spearmen
- Coptic Archers
- Alexandrian Guard (Heavy Infantry)

You will have to use the ruler designer as there is no Coptic ruler in Egypt.

Github : https://github.com/pierresaid/coptic-culture-ck3
