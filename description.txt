Coptic culture mod based on the More Bookmarks+ but with copts being in the byzantine culture group.

Spritual Traditions.
Culture traditions : 
- Strong Believers
- Agrarian
- Monastic Communities
- Expert Artisans

You will have to use the ruler designer as there is no Coptic ruler in Egypt.

Github : https://github.com/pierresaid/coptic-culture-ck3
